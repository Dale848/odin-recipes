A delicious Langos covered with sourcream and cheese

Ingredients

  • 500g flour
  • 1 cube fresh yeast
  • 300ml lukewarm milk
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2tbsp oil
  • Oil for frying (plenty, sunflower works well)
  • toppings: garlic oil, sour cream, grated cheese

Steps

  1. Dissolve the yeast with sugar in the lukewarm milk, let sit for 10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Put flour and salt in a large bowl
  3. Add the yeast-milk mixture and oil to the flour, knead into a smooth, elastic dough
  4. Covert the dough and let it rise in a warm spot for about 1 hour, until double in size.
  5. Divide the dough into 4 prtions on a floured surface.
  6. Shape each portion into a flat, oval disc about 1cm thick
  7. Heat oil in a pot or pan to about 180°C
  8. Fry the discs one at a time in the hot oil, about 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  9. Let the langos drain on paper towels
  10. Serve immediately - classically brushed with garlic oil, then topped with sour cream and cheese.
