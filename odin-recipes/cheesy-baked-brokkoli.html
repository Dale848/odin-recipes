Cheesy baked broccoli

Home

Ingredients

  • 800g broccoli (cut into florets)
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 150g grated cheese (Gruyere or cheddar work well)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 200ml milk
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • Optional: breadcrumbs for topping

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 200°C
  2. Blanch the broccoli florets in salted boiling water for 3-4minutes, then drain well.
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat, add minced garlic and saute briefly
  4. Stir in the flour and cook for 1 minute to from a roux
  5. Gradually whisk in the milk and cream, stirring constantly until smooth.
  6. Simmer the Sauce for 3-5minutes until thickened, then season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  7. Stir in half of the grated cheese until melted.
  8. place the broccoli in a baking dish and pour the cheese sauce over it.
  9. Top with the remaining cheese (and breadcrump if using)
  10. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  11. Let rest for a few minutes before serving.
